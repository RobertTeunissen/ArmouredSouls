## Overview

Without Training Academies, every robot attribute is hard-capped at **level 10**. Academies are the only way to raise those caps, unlocking progressively higher attribute levels up to the absolute maximum of **50**. There are four academies, each governing one category of attributes.

![Training academy system](/images/guide/facilities/training-academy-system.png)

This article covers the academy system from a facilities perspective — costs, operating expenses, and investment strategy. For a detailed breakdown of which attributes each academy controls and build-specific prioritization, see the [Training Academies robot guide](/guide/robots/training-academies).

## The Four Academies

| Academy | Category Controlled | Attributes | Cap Range |
|---------|-------------------|------------|-----------|
| **Combat Training Academy** | Combat Systems | 6 attributes | 10 → 50 |
| **Defense Training Academy** | Defensive Systems | 5 attributes | 10 → 50 |
| **Mobility Training Academy** | Chassis & Mobility | 5 attributes | 10 → 50 |
| **AI Training Academy** | AI Processing + Team Coordination | 7 attributes | 10 → 50 |

All four academies share identical pricing, level progression, and prestige gates. The only difference is which attributes they unlock. All 23 attributes controlled by these academies are active in the combat simulator — including AI Processing (decision-making, adaptation, pressure performance) and Team Coordination (sync volleys, shield regen boost, wall-bracing).

## Level Progression

Each academy level raises the attribute cap for its entire category:

| Academy Level | Attribute Cap | Prestige Required | Upgrade Cost | Operating Cost/Day |
|--------------|--------------|-------------------|-------------|-------------------|
| 0 (none) | 10 | — | — | ₡0 |
| 1 | 15 | — | ₡100,000 | ₡250 |
| 2 | 20 | — | ₡200,000 | ₡500 |
| 3 | 25 | 2,000 | ₡300,000 | ₡750 |
| 4 | 30 | — | ₡400,000 | ₡1,000 |
| 5 | 35 | 4,000 | ₡500,000 | ₡1,250 |
| 6 | 40 | — | ₡600,000 | ₡1,500 |
| 7 | 42 | 7,000 | ₡700,000 | ₡1,750 |
| 8 | 45 | — | ₡800,000 | ₡2,000 |
| 9 | 48 | 10,000 | ₡900,000 | ₡2,250 |
| 10 | 50 | 15,000 | ₡1,000,000 | ₡2,500 |

```callout-info
Prestige gates appear at Levels 3, 5, 7, 9, and 10. Your Prestige is checked but never spent — once you reach the threshold, you can purchase the upgrade if you have the Credits.
```

## Cost Summary

**Single academy (Level 0 → 10)**:
- Total upgrade cost: ₡5,500,000
- Daily operating cost at max: ₡2,500

**All four academies (Level 0 → 10)**:
- Total upgrade cost: ₡22,000,000
- Daily operating cost at max: ₡10,000
- Prestige required: 15,000

```callout-warning
Running all four academies at Level 10 costs ₡10,000/day in operating fees alone. Make sure your income can support this before pushing all academies to max. Most players only need 2–3 academies at high levels.
```

## Academy vs Training Facility

These two facilities are often confused but serve completely different purposes:

| Facility | Purpose | Effect |
|----------|---------|--------|
| **Training Facility** | Reduces attribute upgrade *costs* | Up to 90% discount on upgrade prices |
| **Training Academies** | Raises attribute level *caps* | From 10 up to 50 |

You need both for efficient robot development. The Training Facility makes upgrades affordable; academies make higher levels possible.

```callout-tip
Buy the Training Facility first — it's cheaper and immediately saves you money on every upgrade. Then invest in academies to unlock higher caps once you can afford the upgrades to reach them.
```

## Investment Priority by Build

Not every build needs all four academies equally. Focus your investment on the academy that matches your primary strategy:

| Build Archetype | Primary Academy | Secondary | Notes |
|----------------|----------------|-----------|-------|
| Tank | Defense | Mobility | Armor and shields are your core stats |
| Glass Cannon | Combat | Mobility | Max damage, with hull HP as backup |
| Speed Demon | Mobility | Combat | Evasion and attack speed first |
| Counter Striker | Defense | Combat | Counter Protocols need high caps |
| Sniper | Combat | AI | Targeting, Critical Systems, and Combat Algorithms |
| Melee Brawler | Mobility | Combat | Servo Motors and Hydraulic Systems for close-range dominance |
