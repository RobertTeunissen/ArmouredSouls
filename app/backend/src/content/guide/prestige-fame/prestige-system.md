## Overview

**Prestige** is your stable's permanent reputation score. It represents your overall success, history, and standing in the Armoured Souls community. Prestige is earned through victories and milestones, never spent, and never decreases. It serves as the unlock threshold for facility upgrades, tournament access, and cosmetic content.

Think of Prestige as your stable's lifetime achievement record. Every win adds to it, and the higher it climbs, the more doors it opens.

![Prestige rank progression](/images/guide/prestige-fame/prestige-rank-progression.png)

```callout-info
Prestige is stable-level — one value shared across your entire stable. It doesn't matter which robot wins the battle; the Prestige goes to your stable. This is different from [Fame](/guide/prestige-fame/fame-system), which is tracked per-robot.
```

## How You Earn Prestige

### Battle Victories

Your primary source of Prestige. Every time one of your robots wins a league battle, your stable earns Prestige based on the league tier:

| League Tier | Prestige per Win |
|-------------|-----------------|
| Bronze | +5 |
| Silver | +10 |
| Gold | +20 |
| Platinum | +30 |
| Diamond | +50 |
| Champion | +75 |

Only victories earn Prestige. Draws and losses award nothing. This means climbing to higher leagues is doubly rewarding — you earn more per win and face tougher competition that pushes you to improve.

```callout-tip
A single Champion-tier win earns as much Prestige as 15 Bronze-tier wins. Promoting your robots to higher leagues is the fastest way to accumulate Prestige.
```

### Tournament Performance

Tournaments award Prestige for each battle won, scaling with tournament size and round progression. The deeper you go, the more Prestige each win earns. Winning the finals awards a massive **+500 championship Prestige bonus** on top of the normal amount.

Tournament Prestige provides major "jumps" in your progression — a single championship run can be worth more than dozens of regular league battles. See [Tournament Rewards](/guide/tournaments/rewards) for the full breakdown.

### Milestone Achievements

One-time Prestige bonuses are awarded when you reach notable milestones:

- **ELO milestones** — When your first robot reaches ELO thresholds (1500, 1750, 2000, 2250, 2500)
- **Win count milestones** — Total wins across all robots (100, 250, 500, 1000, 2500, 5000)
- **Championship milestones** — First tournament win, 10 tournament wins, championship titles

Milestones are one-time awards — they don't repeat. They provide memorable progression moments and alternative paths to Prestige beyond grinding battles.

## What Prestige Unlocks

### Facility Upgrade Gates

Many facility upgrades require a minimum Prestige threshold. Your Prestige is checked but never spent — once you reach the threshold, you can purchase the upgrade if you also have the Credits.

Key Prestige gates:

| Prestige Threshold | What It Unlocks |
|-------------------|----------------|
| 1,000 | Several facilities at Level 4, Booking Office Level 1 (not yet implemented) |
| 2,000 | Training Academies Level 3, Research Lab Level 4 |
| 5,000 | Multiple facilities at Level 7, Booking Office Level 3 (not yet implemented) |
| 10,000 | Multiple facilities at Level 9, Booking Office Level 4 (not yet implemented) |
| 15,000 | Training Academies Level 10, Research Lab Level 9 |
| 25,000 | Booking Office Level 7 (not yet implemented), Hall of Fame access |
| 50,000 | Booking Office Level 10 (not yet implemented) |

See [Facility Progression & ROI](/guide/facilities/facility-progression) for the complete prestige gate reference.

```callout-warning
Prestige gates can block your progression if you focus too heavily on spending Credits without winning battles. Make sure your robots are actively competing and winning to keep your Prestige growing alongside your facility investments.
```

### Income Multipliers

Higher Prestige directly increases your earnings. See [Prestige Income Effects](/guide/prestige-fame/prestige-income) for the full breakdown.

### Tournament Access

Tournaments are open to all robots that meet the [eligibility requirements](/guide/tournaments/eligibility) — currently just having the correct weapons equipped. There are no Prestige gates on tournament entry. The [Booking Office](/guide/facilities/facility-overview) facility is planned to add tiered tournament access in a future update, but is not yet implemented.

## Prestige Growth Expectations

Here's roughly how long it takes to reach key Prestige thresholds, assuming consistent play:

| Prestige Target | Approximate Battles (Bronze) | Approximate Battles (Gold) |
|----------------|-----------------------------|-----------------------------|
| 1,000 | ~200 wins | ~50 wins |
| 5,000 | ~1,000 wins | ~250 wins |
| 10,000 | ~2,000 wins | ~500 wins |
| 25,000 | ~5,000 wins | ~1,250 wins |

These estimates don't include tournament bonuses or milestone achievements, which can significantly accelerate your progression.

```callout-tip
Don't stress about Prestige — it grows naturally as you play. Focus on promoting your robots to higher leagues and the Prestige will follow. The system rewards consistent play over time.
```
