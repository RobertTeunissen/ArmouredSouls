## Overview

Your stable's Prestige score is displayed alongside a **rank title** that reflects your standing in the Armoured Souls community. There are six ranks, each representing a tier of achievement and unlocking progressively more content and benefits.

![Prestige rank progression](/images/guide/prestige-fame/prestige-rank-progression.png)

## The Six Prestige Ranks

| Rank | Prestige Range | What It Represents |
|------|---------------|-------------------|
| **Novice** | 0 – 999 | A new stable finding its footing. No special benefits yet. |
| **Established** | 1,000 – 4,999 | A stable that's proven it can compete. First facility gates open. |
| **Veteran** | 5,000 – 9,999 | A respected stable with a track record of success. Income bonuses begin. |
| **Elite** | 10,000 – 24,999 | A top-tier stable known across the leagues. Significant economic advantages. |
| **Champion** | 25,000 – 49,999 | Among the most successful stables in the game. Hall of Fame access. |
| **Legendary** | 50,000+ | An iconic stable with an unmatched legacy. Maximum benefits unlocked. |

```callout-info
Your rank title is displayed next to your stable name in leaderboards, battle results, and your profile. It's a visible badge of your stable's history and achievement.
```

## Rank Progression

### Novice (0 – 999)

Every stable starts here. You're building your first robots, learning the mechanics, and fighting in Bronze league. No Prestige-gated content is locked behind this rank — everything available at 0 Prestige is yours from the start.

**Goal**: Win consistently in Bronze/Silver to reach 1,000 Prestige and unlock your first facility gates.

### Established (1,000 – 4,999)

Your stable has proven it can compete. At 1,000 Prestige, the +10% battle winnings bonus kicks in and several facility Level 4 upgrades become available.

**Key unlocks at this rank**:
- +10% battle winnings bonus
- Repair Bay, Training Facility, Roster Expansion at Level 4
- Booking Office Level 1 (not yet implemented)
- Streaming Studio Level 4

### Veteran (5,000 – 9,999)

A respected stable with a solid track record. The +20% battle winnings bonus applies at 5,000 Prestige, and multiple facilities unlock Level 7 upgrades.

**Key unlocks at this rank**:
- +20% battle winnings bonus
- Multiple facilities at Level 7
- Booking Office Level 3 (not yet implemented)
- Merchandising income scaling becomes meaningful

### Elite (10,000 – 24,999)

A top-tier stable. The +30% battle winnings bonus applies, and most facilities can reach Level 9. Your merchandising income is now doubled compared to a Novice stable.

**Key unlocks at this rank**:
- +30% battle winnings bonus
- Most facilities at Level 9
- Training Academies at Level 9 (attribute cap 48)
- Booking Office Level 4 (not yet implemented)

### Champion (25,000 – 49,999)

Among the most successful stables in the game. The +40% battle winnings bonus applies, and you gain access to the Hall of Fame.

**Key unlocks at this rank**:
- +40% battle winnings bonus
- Booking Office Level 7 (not yet implemented)
- Streaming Studio Level 9

### Legendary (50,000+)

An iconic stable with an unmatched legacy. Maximum income bonuses, World Championship access, and every facility fully unlockable.

**Key unlocks at this rank**:
- +50% battle winnings bonus (maximum)
- Booking Office Level 10 (not yet implemented)
- Streaming Studio Level 10
- All facility gates cleared

```callout-tip
Reaching Legendary is a long-term goal — it takes thousands of battles or significant tournament success. Don't rush it. Each rank along the way provides meaningful benefits that compound over time.
```

## Rank Benefits Summary

| Rank | Battle Bonus | Merchandising Scaling | Facility Access |
|------|-------------|----------------------|----------------|
| Novice | None | Minimal | Levels 1–3 |
| Established | None | Small | + Level 4 gates |
| Veteran | +5% | Moderate | + Level 7 gates |
| Elite | +10% | Significant | + Level 9 gates |
| Champion | +15% | Substantial | + Hall of Fame |
| Legendary | +20% | Maximum | All gates cleared |
