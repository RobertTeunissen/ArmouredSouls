## Overview

Prestige doesn't just unlock facilities — it directly increases your stable's income through two mechanisms: a **battle winnings bonus** and **merchandising revenue scaling**. The higher your Prestige, the more you earn from every battle and every cycle of merchandising income.

![Prestige income impact](/images/guide/prestige-fame/prestige-income-impact.png)

## Battle Winnings Bonus

At certain Prestige thresholds, your stable earns a percentage bonus on all battle winnings. This applies to every league battle and tournament match across your entire roster.

| Prestige Threshold | Battle Winnings Bonus |
|-------------------|----------------------|
| Below 1,000 | No bonus |
| 1,000+ (Established) | +10% to all battle winnings |
| 5,000+ (Veteran) | +20% to all battle winnings |
| 10,000+ (Elite) | +30% to all battle winnings |
| 25,000+ (Champion) | +40% to all battle winnings |
| 50,000+ (Legendary) | +50% to all battle winnings |

The bonus is applied after the base reward is calculated. It stacks with league tier scaling — a Champion-tier robot in a Legendary-prestige stable earns 50% more per battle than the same robot in a Novice-prestige stable.

### Impact Examples

Consider a Gold-tier robot earning a mid-range victory (₡45,000 base):

| Prestige Level | Bonus | Actual Earnings |
|---------------|-------|----------------|
| Novice (0) | 0% | ₡45,000 |
| Established (1,000) | +10% | ₡49,500 |
| Veteran (5,000) | +20% | ₡54,000 |
| Elite (10,000) | +30% | ₡58,500 |
| Champion (25,000) | +40% | ₡63,000 |
| Legendary (50,000) | +50% | ₡67,500 |

The difference seems modest per battle, but it compounds across all your robots and every cycle. A stable with 5 robots each fighting daily at +50% earns significantly more over time.

```callout-tip
The battle winnings bonus applies to every robot in your stable, every battle, every cycle. Even the +10% bonus at Established rank adds up to thousands of extra Credits per cycle if you're running multiple robots.
```

## Merchandising Revenue Scaling

Prestige also scales your [merchandising income](/guide/economy/merchandising). Higher Prestige means a larger, more dedicated fanbase — and more fans means more merchandise sales.

The relationship between Prestige and merchandising is continuous and progressive:

| Prestige Level | Merchandising Impact |
|---------------|---------------------|
| 0 | Baseline merchandising income |
| 5,000 | Merchandising income increased by roughly 50% |
| 10,000 | Merchandising income roughly doubled |
| 25,000 | Merchandising income roughly tripled |
| 50,000 | Merchandising income roughly 6× baseline |

The scaling is smooth — there are no sudden jumps. Every point of Prestige contributes a small incremental increase to your merchandising revenue.

```callout-info
Merchandising scaling makes the [Merchandising Hub](/guide/facilities/facility-overview) facility increasingly valuable as your Prestige grows. A Level 5 Merchandising Hub at 10,000 Prestige generates far more income than the same facility at 1,000 Prestige.
```

### Combined Income Effect

The real power of Prestige income effects comes from combining both bonuses:

| Prestige Rank | Battle Bonus | Merchandising Scaling | Overall Impact |
|--------------|-------------|----------------------|----------------|
| Novice | None | Minimal | Baseline earnings |
| Established | +10% battles | Small boost | Early income growth |
| Veteran | +20% battles | ~50% more merchandising | Noticeable income growth |
| Elite | +30% battles | ~100% more merchandising | Significant advantage |
| Champion | +40% battles | ~150% more merchandising | Major economic edge |
| Legendary | +50% battles | ~500% more merchandising | Maximum earnings |

```callout-warning
Don't neglect Prestige growth in favor of short-term spending. The income multipliers compound over time — a stable that reaches Elite Prestige early will out-earn a higher-spending but lower-Prestige stable within a few hundred cycles.
```

## Prestige Income vs Fame Income

Your stable has two reputation-driven income streams:

| Income Type | Driven By | Scope | Consistency |
|------------|-----------|-------|-------------|
| Battle winnings bonus | Prestige | All robots | Every battle |
| Merchandising | Prestige | Stable-wide | Every cycle |
| Streaming revenue | Fame | Per-robot | Per battle fought |

Prestige-based income (battle bonus + merchandising) rewards your stable's overall reputation. Fame-based income (streaming) rewards individual robot performance. A well-rounded stable invests in both.

```callout-tip
In the early game, focus on winning battles to build Prestige naturally. The income bonuses kick in at 1,000 Prestige (Established rank) and grow from there. By mid game, the combined effect of battle bonuses and merchandising scaling can add 30–50% to your total income.
```
