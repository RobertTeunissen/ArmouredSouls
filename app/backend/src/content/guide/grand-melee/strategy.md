## Build Archetypes

Grand Melee rewards different builds than 1v1 combat. With 19 opponents on the field, survivability and target selection matter more than raw burst damage. Three archetypes dominate:

### The Vulture

- **Key attributes**: High servoMotors, high threatAnalysis
- **Weapon**: Long-range
- **Strategy**: Stay at the edges, avoid early engagements, let others weaken each other, then pick off damaged targets. High servo speed enables escape when pressured; high threat analysis identifies the weakest target in the field.
- **Strengths**: Consistent top-10 finishes, avoids early elimination
- **Weakness**: Rarely gets 1st place — fresh targets run out in the final phase

### The Tank

- **Key attributes**: High hullIntegrity, high armorPlating
- **Weapon**: Mid-range
- **Strategy**: Absorb chip damage from multiple sources, outlast glass cannons that crumble under crossfire. Mid-range weapons allow engagement without committing to close range.
- **Strengths**: Survives multi-robot focus fire, strong in the late game
- **Weakness**: Lower kill rate means less damage-based tiebreaker advantage

### The Brawler

- **Key attributes**: High servoMotors, high hydraulicSystems
- **Weapon**: Melee
- **Strategy**: Close distance quickly, burst down a target, move to the next. High servo speed mitigates the melee disadvantage of needing to close range. Hydraulic bonuses reward aggressive positioning.
- **Strengths**: High kill rate, strong damage tiebreaker, dominant in late-game close quarters
- **Weakness**: Vulnerable to crossfire during approach, multiple ranged robots can focus you down

## Attribute Value Shifts vs 1v1

Some attributes matter significantly more (or less) in Grand Melee compared to 1v1 League:

| Attribute | 1v1 Value | Grand Melee Value | Why |
|-----------|-----------|-------------------|-----|
| servoMotors | Medium | Very High | Escape, reposition, chase weakened targets |
| threatAnalysis | Medium | Very High | Target selection among 19 opponents is critical |
| hullIntegrity | Medium | Very High | Surviving multiple fights requires raw HP |
| armorPlating | Medium | High | Chip damage from many sources adds up |
| hydraulicSystems | Medium | Situational | Melee is risky in open-field FFA but strong in late game |

## Positioning Tips

### Engage Early for Damage Credit

Don't wait too long to deal damage. If the battle hits the time limit, the **total damage tiebreaker** determines placement among same-tick eliminations. Robots that dealt zero damage lose every tiebreaker.

### Avoid Being Caught in Crossfire

Positioning between two engaged robots is dangerous — both may redirect attacks toward you. Stay on the periphery of fights rather than inserting yourself between combatants.

### Watch the Clock

At 120 seconds, survivors are ranked by HP%. If you're healthy and in the top group, sometimes **disengaging** in the final seconds is the right call — protect your HP% rather than risking a fight that could drop your placement.
