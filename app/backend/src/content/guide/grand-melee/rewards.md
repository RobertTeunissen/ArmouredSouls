## Credits

All Grand Melee participants earn credits. The base payout is **2.5× the KotH base** multiplied by a placement multiplier. Higher placements earn significantly more credits.

Even robots that finish in the bottom half receive a **participation floor** — you'll never walk away empty-handed.

## Fame

**Top 10 finishers** earn Fame from Grand Melee. Fame rewards are **tier-scaled** — placing well in higher tiers earns more Fame than the same placement in lower tiers.

Robots finishing 11th–20th do not earn Fame from that battle.

## Prestige

**Top 3 finishers** earn Prestige. The winner receives the largest Prestige award, with 2nd and 3rd receiving progressively smaller amounts.

### Winner's Bonus

The 1st place finisher receives a **+50% Prestige bonus** if they end the battle with more than 50% HP remaining. Dominating a 20-robot free-for-all while staying healthy is the ultimate display of superiority.

## Reward Summary

| Placement | Credits | Fame | Prestige |
|-----------|---------|------|----------|
| 1st | 2.5× base × top multiplier | ✅ Tier-scaled | ✅ + 50% HP bonus if >50% HP |
| 2nd–3rd | 2.5× base × placement multiplier | ✅ Tier-scaled | ✅ |
| 4th–10th | 2.5× base × placement multiplier | ✅ Tier-scaled | — |
| 11th–20th | 2.5× base × low multiplier (floor) | — | — |

## Participation Floor

Every robot that enters Grand Melee receives at least the floor credit reward regardless of placement. This ensures that even a last-place finish contributes to your economic progress.
