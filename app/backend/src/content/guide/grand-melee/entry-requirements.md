## Booking Office Subscription

Grand Melee is a **subscription-gated** event. Your robot must have an active Booking Office subscription for the Grand Melee event to be eligible for matchmaking.

You can manage subscriptions from the Robot Detail page or the Booking Office overview. Subscribing is free — it only uses one of your per-robot subscription slots.

## Weapon Readiness

Your robot must have **at least one weapon equipped** to be eligible. Robots without a weapon cannot enter combat and will be excluded from matchmaking.

## Minimum Robot Threshold

A Grand Melee match requires a **minimum of 8 robots** per tier instance to fire. If fewer than 8 eligible robots are available in a tier at 17:00 UTC, no match is created for that tier instance that day.

When 8–19 robots are available, the match runs with fewer than 20 participants. The scoring system adjusts — placements are still ranked by elimination order, but fewer placement slots are filled.

## Starting Tier

All robots begin their Grand Melee competitive standing in **Bronze tier**. As you accumulate LP through strong placements, you'll promote into higher tiers where competition is fiercer and rewards are greater.

## No Other Prerequisites

There are no level requirements, no minimum ELO, and no prestige gates. Any robot with a weapon and a subscription can enter Grand Melee from day one.
