## What is Grand Melee?

Grand Melee is a **20-robot free-for-all elimination** battle mode. Twenty robots enter the arena simultaneously and fight until only one remains standing. There are no teams, no alliances — every robot is an opponent.

Unlike 1v1 League or KotH zone control, Grand Melee is pure elimination combat. The last robot standing wins.

## How It Works

1. **20 robots** are matched together based on tier and LP standing
2. All robots spawn in the arena at randomized positions
3. Combat begins immediately — robots select targets, engage, and eliminate each other
4. When a robot's HP reaches zero, it's eliminated and assigned a placement based on elimination order
5. The **last robot standing** takes 1st place and the full winner's reward

The battle has a **120-second time limit**. If multiple robots survive when time expires, remaining survivors are ranked by their current HP percentage — the robot with the most HP remaining takes the highest placement among survivors.

## Daily Schedule

Grand Melee runs **daily at 17:00 UTC**. One match fires per tier instance (provided enough robots are subscribed). Results are processed immediately after the battle completes.

## How to Enter

Grand Melee participation requires a **Booking Office subscription**. Navigate to the Booking Office (or your robot's subscription panel) and subscribe the robot to the Grand Melee event.

Once subscribed, your robot will be automatically entered into the next Grand Melee matchmaking cycle at 17:00 UTC. No further action is required — the system handles scheduling and grouping.
