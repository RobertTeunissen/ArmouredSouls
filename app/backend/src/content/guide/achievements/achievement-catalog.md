## Overview

Achievements are grouped into five categories. Categories are for reference only — on the achievements page, all achievements appear in a single list with filter and sort controls.

Each achievement has a pop culture reference in its name. See if you can spot them all.

## 🤖 Combat — Robot-Level

Triggered by individual robot performance. The achievement is earned by your stable through a specific robot.

| Name | Tier | How to Earn |
|------|------|-------------|
| **"I'll Be Back"** | 🟢 Easy | Win your first battle |
| **"Danger, Will Robinson!"** | 🟢 Easy | Lose your first battle |
| **"Bite My Shiny Metal Badge"** | 🟢 Easy | Complete 10 battles on one robot |
| **"By Your Command"** | 🔵 Medium | Win 25 battles on one robot |
| **"Hasta La Vista, Baby"** | 🔵 Medium | Destroy an opponent (0 HP) 25 times on one robot |
| **"I Am Iron Man"** | 🟠 Hard | Win 50 battles on one robot |
| **"Exterminate!"** | 🟠 Hard | Destroy opponents 50 times on one robot |
| **"I, Robot"** | 🔴 Very Hard | Win 100 battles on one robot |
| **"Flawless Victory"** | 🟢 Easy | Win a battle with 100% HP remaining |
| **"It's Just a Flesh Wound"** | 🔵 Medium | Win a battle with less than 10% HP remaining |
| **"Never Tell Me the Odds"** | 🟠 Hard | Win against an opponent 150+ ELO above you |
| **"Resistance Is Futile"** | 🟠 Hard | Win 10 consecutive league battles |
| **"Look What They Need..."** | 🔴 Very Hard | Win 15 consecutive league battles |
| **"Do You Yield?"** | 🟢 Easy | Force an opponent to yield for the first time |
| **"I Didn't Hear No Bell"** | 🟢 Easy | Win a battle after losing the previous one on the same robot |
| **"Wax On, Wax Off"** | 🟢 Easy | Fight 25 practice arena battles |
| **"Autobots, Roll Out!"** | 🟠 Hard | Win at least one battle in each mode: league, KotH, tag team, and tournament |

```callout-tip
"Flawless Victory" is one of the easiest achievements to earn early — if your robot significantly outclasses an opponent, you might take zero damage. Keep an eye on your HP after battles.
```

## 💰 Economy & Stable — User-Level

Triggered by stable-wide economic activity, facility ownership, and roster management.

| Name | Tier | How to Earn |
|------|------|-------------|
| **"Hello World"** | 🟢 Easy | Complete onboarding |
| **"The Odds Are 725 to 1"** | 🟢 Easy | Earn ₡100,000 total from battles |
| **"Show Me the Money"** | 🟠 Hard | Accumulate ₡5,000,000 in your balance at once |
| **"Scrooge McDuck"** | 🟠 Hard | Earn ₡25,000,000 lifetime from all sources |
| **"Buy the Dip"** | 🟢 Easy | Purchase your first weapon |
| **"Arsenal"** | 🔵 Medium | Own 10 weapons simultaneously |
| **"Mall Cop"** | 🟢 Easy | Buy your first shield |
| **"Pimp My Bot"** | 🟢 Easy | Upgrade any robot attribute for the first time |
| **"Fully Operational"** | 🟠 Hard | Reach an effective stat of 50 on any attribute |
| **"The Architect"** | 🟠 Hard | Own 3 facilities at level 3+ |
| **"Megacorp"** | 🔴 Very Hard | Own 5 facilities at level 3+ |
| **"Skynet Is Online"** | 🔵 Medium | Own 3 robots simultaneously |
| **"Robot Army"** | 🟠 Hard | Own 5 robots simultaneously |
| **"Rise of the Machines"** | 🔴 Very Hard | Own 7 robots simultaneously |
| **"Fine-Tuned Machine"** | 🟢 Easy | Allocate tuning points on any robot for the first time |
| **"Overclocked"** | 🔵 Medium | Allocate 50+ tuning points on a single robot |

```callout-tip
"Fully Operational" checks your robot's **effective** stat — that's base + weapon bonuses + tuning + loadout bonus + stance modifier. You don't need to upgrade the base attribute to 50; stacking bonuses from multiple sources counts.
```

## 🌟 Prestige & Fame — Mixed Level

Triggered by prestige thresholds (stable-level) and fame thresholds (robot-level). These achievements turn the reputation systems into celebrated milestones.

| Name | Tier | How to Earn | Scope |
|------|------|-------------|-------|
| **"Established"** | 🔵 Medium | Reach 1,000 prestige | Stable |
| **"Veteran"** | 🟠 Hard | Reach 5,000 prestige | Stable |
| **"Elite"** | 🔴 Very Hard | Reach 10,000 prestige | Stable |
| **"Legendary"** | 🔴 Very Hard | Reach 25,000 prestige | Stable |
| **"15 Minutes of Fame"** | 🟢 Easy | Robot reaches 100 fame (Known tier) | Robot |
| **"Famous Last Words"** | 🔵 Medium | Robot reaches 500 fame (Famous tier) | Robot |
| **"Legendary Status"** | 🟠 Hard | Robot reaches 2,500 fame (Legendary tier) | Robot |
| **"Mythical Proportions"** | 🔴 Very Hard | Robot reaches 5,000 fame (Mythical tier) | Robot |
| **"Influencer"** | 🟢 Easy | Earn ₡100,000 total streaming revenue | Stable |
| **"Content Creator"** | 🟠 Hard | Earn ₡1,000,000 total streaming revenue | Stable |

```callout-info
Prestige achievements are stable-level — your total prestige across all activity. Fame achievements are robot-level — each robot's individual fame score is checked separately.
```

## 🎲 Style & Quirky — Robot-Level

Triggered by specific playstyle choices, loadout configurations, and unusual battle outcomes.

| Name | Tier | How to Earn |
|------|------|-------------|
| **"Bleep Bloop"** | 🟢 Easy | Equip a robot with an energy weapon |
| **"Get to the Choppa"** | 🟢 Easy | Equip a robot with a ballistic weapon |
| **"Sword Art Online"** | 🟢 Easy | Equip a robot with a melee weapon |
| **"Dual Wielding Intensifies"** | 🔵 Medium | Win 10 battles with dual-wield loadout |
| **"Turtle Mode Activated"** | 🔵 Medium | Win 10 battles on defensive stance |
| **"LEEROY JENKINS!"** | 🔵 Medium | Win 10 battles on offensive stance |
| **"Perfectly Balanced"** | 🔵 Medium | Win 10 battles on balanced stance |
| **"No Retreat, No Surrender"** | 🟠 Hard | Win a battle with yield threshold set to 0% |
| **"Dead or Alive, You're Coming With Me"** | 🔵 Medium | Force 10 opponents to yield |

```callout-tip
The weapon type achievements (Bleep Bloop, Get to the Choppa, Sword Art Online) are among the easiest to earn — just equip the right weapon type. You'll likely get all three in your first few sessions.
```

## Secret Achievements

There are also **secret achievements** hidden throughout the game. They appear as "???" on the achievements page until you earn them. Secret achievements are triggered by unusual or unexpected gameplay moments — we won't spoil them here.

```callout-info
Secret achievements award ₡50,000 and +50 prestige by default. They're marked with a purple badge border once unlocked.
```
