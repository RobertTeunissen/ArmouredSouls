## Overview

Every robot attribute starts capped at **level 10**. No matter how many Credits you have, you can't push an attribute past 10 without investing in the corresponding **Training Academy**. There are four academies, each controlling the caps for a specific category of attributes.

Training Academies are facilities you purchase and upgrade with Credits. Each academy level raises the attribute cap for its category, unlocking progressively higher levels up to the absolute maximum of 50.

![Training academy progression](/images/guide/robots/training-academy-progression.png)

## The Four Training Academies

Each academy controls the attribute caps for one category of attributes:

| Academy | Controls | Attributes Affected |
|---------|----------|-------------------|
| **Combat Training Academy** | 🔴 Combat Systems (6 attributes) | Combat Power, Targeting Systems, Critical Systems, Penetration, Weapon Control, Attack Speed |
| **Defense Training Academy** | 🔵 Defensive Systems (5 attributes) | Armor Plating, Energy Shield Capacity, Evasion Thrusters, Damage Dampeners, Counter Protocols |
| **Mobility Training Academy** | 🟢 Chassis & Mobility (5 attributes) | Hull Integrity, Servo Motors, Gyro Stabilizers, Hydraulic Systems, Power Core |
| **AI Training Academy** | 🟡 AI Processing + 🟣 Team Coordination (7 attributes) | Combat Algorithms, Threat Analysis, Adaptive AI, Logic Cores, Sync Protocols, Support Systems, Formation Tactics |

```callout-info
The AI Training Academy covers both AI Processing and Team Coordination attributes — that's 7 attributes total, making it the broadest academy. If you plan to compete in team modes, this academy unlocks both your decision-making and teamwork capabilities.
```

## Academy Levels and Attribute Caps

All four academies follow the same progression structure. Each level unlocks a higher attribute cap for every attribute in that category:

| Academy Level | Attribute Cap | Prestige Required | Upgrade Cost |
|--------------|--------------|-------------------|-------------|
| 0 (none) | 10 | — | — |
| 1 | 15 | None | ₡100,000 |
| 2 | 20 | None | ₡200,000 |
| 3 | 25 | 2,000 | ₡300,000 |
| 4 | 30 | None | ₡400,000 |
| 5 | 35 | 4,000 | ₡500,000 |
| 6 | 40 | None | ₡600,000 |
| 7 | 42 | 7,000 | ₡700,000 |
| 8 | 45 | None | ₡800,000 |
| 9 | 48 | 10,000 | ₡900,000 |
| 10 | 50 (maximum) | 15,000 | ₡1,000,000 |

Each academy also has a daily operating cost of **₡250 per level** (e.g., Level 5 costs ₡1,250/day).

```callout-warning
Academy levels 3, 5, 7, 9, and 10 require Prestige thresholds. You can't just buy your way to max attributes — you need to earn Prestige through battle victories and tournament performance. Plan your progression accordingly.
```

## Total Investment to Max an Academy

To take a single academy from Level 0 to Level 10 (unlocking the full attribute cap of 50):

- **Total upgrade cost**: ₡5,500,000 (₡100K + ₡200K + ... + ₡1,000K)
- **Total Prestige required**: 15,000 (for the final level)
- **Daily operating cost at max**: ₡2,500/day

To max **all four academies**:

- **Total upgrade cost**: ₡22,000,000
- **Prestige required**: 15,000 (same threshold applies to each)
- **Daily operating cost**: ₡10,000/day

```callout-tip
You don't need to max all four academies at once. Focus on the academy that matches your primary build strategy first. A glass cannon build should prioritize the Combat Training Academy, while a tank build benefits more from the Defense Training Academy.
```

## Academy Investment Strategy

### Early Game Priority

In the early game, your attributes are capped at 10 and you have limited funds. Here's the recommended approach:

1. **Pick one or two academies** that match your robot's build
2. **Get them to Level 1–2** (₡100K–₡300K each) to unlock caps of 15–20
3. **Don't rush to Level 3** — it requires 2,000 Prestige, which takes time to earn

Getting your key attributes from 10 to 15–20 is a significant power boost that's affordable early on.

### Mid Game Expansion

As your income grows and Prestige accumulates:

1. **Push your primary academy to Level 4–5** (caps of 30–35)
2. **Start a second academy** to Level 2–3 for your secondary attributes
3. **Consider the Mobility Academy** — Hull Integrity and Power Core benefit almost every build

### Late Game Completion

With established income streams and high Prestige:

1. **Push primary academy to Level 8–10** for attribute caps of 45–50
2. **Bring secondary academies to Level 5–6** for caps of 35–40
3. **Max the AI Academy** if you're competing in team modes

## Academy and Training Facility Synergy

The [Training Facility](/guide/robots/upgrade-costs) and Training Academies work together but serve different purposes:

| Facility | What It Does |
|----------|-------------|
| **Training Facility** | Reduces the *cost* of attribute upgrades (up to 90% discount) |
| **Training Academies** | Raises the *cap* on attribute levels (from 10 up to 50) |

You need both to build a powerful robot efficiently:
- Academies unlock the ability to upgrade past level 10
- The Training Facility makes those expensive high-level upgrades affordable

```callout-tip
Invest in the Training Facility first (it's cheaper and benefits all attributes immediately), then invest in academies to unlock higher caps. There's no point raising the cap to 30 if you can't afford the upgrades to get there.
```

## Which Academy Should You Prioritize?

The answer depends on your build archetype:

| Build Type | Primary Academy | Secondary Academy | Why |
|-----------|----------------|-------------------|-----|
| **Tank** | Defense | Mobility | Armor, shields, and hull HP are your core |
| **Glass Cannon** | Combat | Mobility | Max damage output, with hull HP as backup |
| **Speed Demon** | Mobility | Combat | Attack speed, evasion, and movement first |
| **Counter Striker** | Defense | Combat | Counter Protocols and Armor Plating are key |
| **Sniper** | Combat | AI | Targeting, Critical Systems, and smart decision-making |
| **Tactical AI** | AI | Combat or Defense | Decision quality drives everything |
| **Team Player** | AI | Defense or Combat | Team Coordination + AI Processing in one academy |
