## Zone Scoring

Points accumulate at **1 point per second** while a robot holds the zone uncontested. Contested or empty zones award zero points. Your Zone Score is the primary metric for placement.

## Kill Bonus

Destroying an opponent awards **5 bonus points** to the attacker. Yielding does not award a kill bonus to anyone. This incentivizes aggressive play and finishing off weakened opponents.

## Win Conditions

A match ends when any of these conditions is met:

1. **Score Threshold** — A robot reaches 30 points (fixed zone) or 45 points (rotating zone). The match ends immediately.
2. **Time Limit** — 150 seconds (fixed) or 210 seconds (rotating) elapse. The robot with the highest Zone Score wins.
3. **Last Standing** — All but one robot are eliminated. A 10-second countdown begins, then the highest scorer wins.

## Tiebreakers

When two or more robots have the same Zone Score, ties are broken by:
1. Total zone occupation time (more time wins)
2. Total damage dealt (more damage wins)

## Last Standing Phase

When only one robot remains alive, the match doesn't end immediately. Instead, a 10-second window opens. The survivor can continue scoring, but the final winner is still determined by highest Zone Score — not by being the last one standing.
