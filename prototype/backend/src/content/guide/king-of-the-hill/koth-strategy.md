## Key Attributes for KotH

### Servo Motors
High servo motors help you reach the zone first and reposition quickly. Essential for rotating zone matches where you need to race to the new zone position.

### Threat Analysis
Affects zone movement bias. At low values (1-10), robots only partially bias toward the zone. At high values (30+), robots strongly prioritize zone control. Also scales target priority weights.

### Combat Algorithms
Robots with combatAlgorithms above 25 can use the **wait-and-enter** tactic: holding position just outside the zone edge when two others are contesting it, then entering once one is eliminated.

### Hull Integrity & Armor Plating
Survivability matters in a 5-6 robot free-for-all. Being eliminated early means a low Zone Score and poor placement. Tanky builds can hold the zone under pressure.

## Tactical Approaches

### Zone Rush
High servo motors, rush to zone immediately. Works well in fixed zone matches where early uncontested scoring is valuable.

### Wait-and-Enter
High combatAlgorithms build. Stay 2 units outside the zone edge while others fight inside. Enter when the zone becomes uncontested or when you can pick off a weakened opponent.

### Zone Brawler
High hull integrity and armor. Enter the zone and fight anyone who contests it. Prioritize the lowest-HP contester to clear the zone quickly.

### Sniper
Stay outside the zone and pick off robots from range. Collect kill bonuses (5 points each) without risking zone contests. Works if you can eliminate enough opponents.
