## Placement Rewards

Rewards are tiered by final placement:

| Placement | Credits | Fame | Prestige |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1st | ₡25,000 | 8 | +15 |
| 2nd | ₡17,500 | 5 | +8 |
| 3rd | ₡10,000 | 3 | +3 |
| 4th-6th | ₡5,000 | 0 | 0 |

The winner's fame is further modified by a performance multiplier: Perfect Victory (100% HP) = 2.0×, Dominating (>80% HP) = 1.5×, Comeback (<20% HP) = 1.25×.

## Zone Dominance Bonus

If a robot's uncontested zone time exceeds **75% of the total uncontested scoring time** in the match, they receive a **25% bonus** on all rewards. This rewards dominant zone control.

## ELO Impact

KotH battles do **not** affect ELO ratings. ELO is used only for matchmaking group balancing. Your robot's league standing is unaffected by KotH results.

## Streaming Revenue

All KotH participants receive streaming revenue, just like league battles. This is awarded regardless of placement.

## Repair Costs

Robots damaged in KotH battles need repairs just like any other battle. Factor repair costs into your KotH strategy — aggressive builds may earn more rewards but also incur higher repair bills.

## Cumulative Stats

KotH tracks separate cumulative statistics: wins, matches, total zone score, zone time, kills, best placement, and win streaks. These feed into the KotH Standings page and Hall of Records.
